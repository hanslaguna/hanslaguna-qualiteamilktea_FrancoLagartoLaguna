FAQs





How to Order:

  1. Visit our official page and contacts us or go to Grab food or Food panda and search for Qualitea Milk Tea.
  2. Contact Us
  3. Tell us your orders.
  4. Review Order details before purchasing.



WHAT KIND OF TEA DO YOU USE?

  • We select only the finest whole leaf tea. After being harvested, handpicked leaves undergo the traditional 12 stages of tea making procedures. Our premium tea leaves such as Ceylon, Jasmine and Mountain tea are sourced in the finest locations of Sri Lanka, Taiwan and China.



WHERE DO YOU SOURCE YOUR FRUITS?

  1. Our fruits are sourced from the best locations, both local and abroad.



HOW LONG WILL MY DRINK STAY FRESH?

  • To ensure quality and freshness, we recommend consuming your drink within two (2) hours.



CAN I STORE MY DRINK IN THE REFRIGERATOR OR FREEZER?

  • Yes. However, for drinks with pearls, we do not recommend doing so, as this will cause the pearls’ texture to change.

DO YOU ACCEPT SENIOR CITIZEN AND PWD DISCOUNTS?

  • We give Senior Citizen and PWD discounts under Republic Act Nos. 9994 (Expanded Senior Citizen Act of 2010) and 10754 (Act of Expanding the Benefits and Privileges of a Person with Disability), respectively. Kindly present your senior citizen or PWD ID before making any payments.



WHAT DO I DO IF I RECEIVE A WRONG ORDER?:

  • Kindly inform our baristas and we will gladly replace your drink. Should you have any further concerns, you may email customerservice@tobistrofoods.com



ARE YOU OPEN FOR FRANCHISE?

  • We are currently not open for any franchising opportunities in the Philippines
